The problem

A market growing 20% a year — and every way to sell in it is broken.

Dubizzle & Facebook groups

Classifieds built for cars and sofas. No in-app payments, no buyer protection — just DMs, haggling, and meeting strangers with cash.

Luxury consignment

The Luxury Closet, Garderobe, Retold — designer-only, commissions up to 40%, and payouts often in store credit. Your everyday wardrobe doesn't qualify.

Flea markets & pop-ups

Rent a table, haul everything to Zabeel Park, stand there all evening. Physical, one day only, and capped at whoever walks past.

Vinted & Depop

The apps that solved this elsewhere don't operate properly here — no shipping integration in the UAE, so sellers end up booking their own couriers anyway.

How it works

List it. Someone buys it. It gets collected. You get paid.

01

Photograph it, list it

Snap a few photos, set a price, post it. Takes less time than writing the WhatsApp message you'd send instead.

02

Someone buys it in-app

Payment happens on Souk, not over a bank transfer with a stranger. The buyer's money is held until the item actually arrives.

03

A rider collects it from your door

No printing labels, no driving to a drop-off point. Someone comes to you, picks it up, takes it to the buyer.

04

You get paid

Once it's delivered, the money lands with you. Souk takes a small buyer protection fee — that's it, no listing fees.
